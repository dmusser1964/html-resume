About Me

I was born and raised in Salt Lake City. I loved any sort of outdoor games, (not an athlete by any stretch of the imagination), but loved running. I played soccer in school and loved to play basketball and took up softball as I got older. The big thing for me was just to be outdoors.

My big love, (outside of my children and grandchildren) is running, particularly trail running. Growing up in Salt Lake we were surrounded by mountains and as a teenager I loved hiking and exploring. In 2017 I ran my first Spartan race, (Spartan Super), with my daughter. I loved it so much I have completed 18 additional Spartan races.

I developed a love for running on the trails. I have completed multiple distances, (5K, 10K, half-marathon and full marathon distances) as well as completing six (6) 50K's. I am going for my first 50-mile race, (in October of this year), and training for my first 100K, (February of 2027).

I have been in manufacturing most of my life. My strength is that I can break down problems and find solutions. I currently work in the solar industry and have a passion for developing software applications. Currently I'm studying Python and Web Development. I love organizing data and presenting it in a way that makes it easier to understand.

I strive to continue my quest for knowledge, learning new skills along the way and maintain my honesty, integer and work ethic.
